We're modernizing the GCTools to serve you better.

GCcollab
A collaboration and networking platform available to all orders of government, post-secondary institutions, not-for-profit organizations, as well as their partners and clients by invitation. The first GCTool to be hosted in the cloud, and the first with a mobile app on Apple and Android devices.

The Career Marketplace
A feature within GCcollab that matches members with professional development opportunities.

Collab Message
A messaging service on desktop and mobile that is designed to send direct messages and create private or public channels for open or closed conversations.

Collab Wiki
A platform to collaboratively create and publish content online. It is editable by users and visible to the public.

GCpedia
A wiki-based collaborative workspace and knowledge-sharing platform, similar to Wikipedia, accessible to all federal public servants.

GCconnex
A collaboration and networking platform accessible to federal public servants.

  What's New? Where are we going?
Over the past 10 years, we’ve created collaboration tools for Canadian federal public servants. Now, we’re modernizing those tools to build an ecosystem of applications, a digital workspace, aimed at increasing collaboration and productivity in public service and beyond.
